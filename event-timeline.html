    Koda Kodi

    Post-Wedding Game

    Once couple gets married after the phera's (wedding rounds), they play a game called Koda Kodi.
    Water / Milk is mixed with Sindoor (red dye) in a Thali (Tray).
    In this tray Koda and Kodi's are put for the couple to find them by picking them up turn by turn.
    It is believed that the person who finds more will rule the household.
